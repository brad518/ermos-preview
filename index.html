Straight answers

The questions we know you're asking.

Is it really like ChatGPT?
Yes. Your teams use it like a private ChatGPT, except it reads your own firm's documents and answers from them.
Is our data really safe?
It stays on a box in your office, with no internet path out. The honest answer: your data is as safe as your building, because that's where it lives. We don't claim it's "unhackable". We make it physically unable to leave.
Is it as smart as ChatGPT?
For open-ended general knowledge, a frontier cloud model may edge it. For your work, your matters, your files, your house style, it's grounded in knowledge those models will never see. We'd rather be honest about that than oversell it.
How fast is it?
Fast enough to feel immediate for everyday search and drafting. It runs on dedicated hardware that does nothing else but serve your firm.
What if it breaks?
It's a managed service. If something goes wrong, it's our problem to fix, not yours. That's the whole point of the relationship.
Do we need IT staff?
No. There's nothing for your team to run or maintain. You never touch the technical side. We do.
What does it cost?
Two managed options: Dominion Core from $3,995 one-time then $2,499 a month, and Dominion Growth from $4,995 then $3,999 a month. Both cover your whole firm with unlimited users on a simple month-to-month basis. The deployment section above lists exactly what's included, and your precise scope is confirmed in the consultation.
Are we locked in?
No. Dominion is month-to-month. You stay because it delivers value, not because you're locked in. If it ever stops being the right fit, you can leave, or purchase the appliance for a fixed price and continue running it independently. Either way, your data and knowledge remain yours.
Can we upgrade to Dominion Growth?
Yes. If your team or knowledge base outgrows Core, we upgrade you to Growth in place. Your data, configuration and history carry across. There's no rebuild and no starting again.
Can storage be expanded?
Yes. Knowledge capacity can be increased as your document base grows. We size it with you up front and scale it later if you need more room.
How long does installation take?
Typically your Dominion is live in your office within about two weeks. We install on site, ingest your documents, configure it around how your firm works, and train your team, so it arrives working.
Does Dominion replace our existing IT?
No. Dominion sits alongside your existing systems as a private AI layer. It doesn't replace your IT, your files or your tools; it makes them far more useful. We handle the AI side, and your IT stays as it is.
Can Dominion work with Microsoft 365?
Yes. Dominion connects to Microsoft 365 and your existing document stores, so your team works with the files they already use, without moving anything to the cloud.
Do we own our data?
Completely. Your data, your models and your prompts stay on your premises and belong to you. Nothing is sent out, and there's no lock-in. If the agreement ever ends, what's yours stays with you.
